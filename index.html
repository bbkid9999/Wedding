Ceremony

Love is what I see you as,
As red as a Ruby Rose,
Who stole a heart of innocent blue,
Then turned it into gold,
Bright shining as the stars above,
It Glitters just for you.

No more Roses are Red, Violets are Blue,
Cliches are so not cool,
This Valentine's Day it's easy to say,
I want to marry you someday.

So dear Ruby Rose its Glittering Gold,
Who thanks you for stealing me away,
My heart knew not what a light could do,
Until I first met you.

With that concludes a promise to you,
That Glittering Gold is a heart to hold a rose that only you.
